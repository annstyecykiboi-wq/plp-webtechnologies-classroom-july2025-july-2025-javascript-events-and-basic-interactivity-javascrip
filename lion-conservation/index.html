Lion Facts

Learn about these majestic big cats.

  • Lions live in prides of up to 30 members
  • Males weigh 400-550 lbs, females 260-400 lbs
  • Roar can be heard up to 5 miles away
  • Life expectancy: 10-14 years in the wild
